Why Vega Matters

The Vega ecosystem — Vega, Vega-Lite, Vega-Altair, VegaFusion, vl-convert — is open-source data visualization infrastructure. Charts render natively in any browser or server-side; Vega-Altair brings the same grammar to Python.

  • 44+ million monthly PyPI downloads for Altair
  • 3+ million monthly CDN downloads for Vega and Vega-Lite
  • One of the most widely used visualization stacks in the Python and web ecosystems
  • Trusted by companies, research institutions, data teams, and tool vendors worldwide
Why Sponsorship Is Needed

Vega is maintained by a small volunteer team. Without sustained funding, roadmap progress slows, maintenance becomes harder, and the ecosystem risks stagnation.

Your sponsorship directly unlocks: faster feature development, improved documentation, reliable long-term maintenance, and a healthier ecosystem for everyone who builds on Vega — including companies, startups, research labs, and data teams.

What Your Sponsorship Funds
  • Core maintainers — Dedicated time for maintainers to work on Vega instead of solely putting work in during spare time. Essential for long-term sustainability and accelerated progress.
  • Mentored student contributors — Scoped features under maintainer supervision; real impact on the codebase, valuable student experience, and an onboarding ramp for potential new core contributors. Previous support has sponsored core improvements to the development ecosystem
  • Infrastructure & documentation — Hosting, issue triage, and ongoing documentation improvements.
  • Core-developer or user conference — Where roadmap direction is set and contributors meet; attendance funded for core developers. We hosted a Vega conference in 2023 with sponsorship from the Moore foundation.
